About Lensify

We make prescription lenses for smart glasses — so you never have to choose between seeing clearly and staying connected.

Our Mission

Smart glasses are changing the way we interact with the world. But for millions of people who wear prescription glasses, using them comfortably has been a challenge — until now.

At Lensify, we craft custom prescription lenses specifically designed to fit Ray-Ban Meta AI Glasses, preserving every feature of your frames while giving you the crystal-clear vision you need.

Every lens is made to your exact prescription with Ultra High Index materials, shipped with premium coatings at no extra cost, and backed by our commitment to precision and quality.
